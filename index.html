HOW IT WORKS:

There are 20 spaces on the board. You must reach your profit goal by the time you get back to the beginning, AND your bank account cannot hit $0, or you lose. Roll the 4-sided dice to see how far you move.

On each turn you must pull one of the 84 cards from the DOGE DECK (™)

DOGE DECK (™) Distribution:

  • The Good Tweet (21 cards)
  • The Bad Tweet (21 cards)
  • The Doge Miner (19 cards)
  • Life Happens (18 cards)
  • Robinhood (3 cards)
  • Meme Lord (2 cards)

    • The Good Tweet: The current price of doge rises by a random amount

    The Bad Tweet: The current price of doge goes down by 20%

    The Doge Miner: The number of dogecoins you have goes up OR stays the same (after all you ARE new at this)

    Life Happens: Three of these four cards deplete your bank balance, the other one takes away half your doges, but you won't know why until you pull it (much mystery)

    Robinhood: Takes away ONE of your three lives. Three Robinhood cards before you reach the end and you lose

    Meme Lord: Find the two Meme Lord cards during your game and you win! ...almost... you still have to reach the finish with at least one life and a non-zero bank balance
